About Me

I'm Xiaomeng Huang, currently a Master in Statistics student at Columbia University. I also have a bachelor degree in statistics from University of Minnesota.

Statistics is all about inference and prediction, extracting information from the noise,finding patterns from the chaos, and utilize those to guide the next step or make predictions. 7 years studying that, statistics is not only my major, but also the subject that influences the way I think. Other than doing data anlaysis, as someone who loves arts, I also enjoy spending my time on data visualization. I find it the perfect way for me to combine my major and my interest.
