Workout Intensity on Changes in BMI


This project is the first consultant project I did. Together with Stefani Weimholt and Penny Zhang, we helped two investigators from medical school to study the objective if high intense workout and low intense workout have different impact on changes in BMI.

We used a virtual enviroment, which contains 3 islands, serveral cities and different population, to conduct the experiment.

After collecting, cleaning, and analyzing the data, we concluded that, in terms of effects of high intensity workout (HIWO) and low intensity workout (LIWO) on change in BMI, the difference is statistical significant but not clinical significant. For further invesitgation, a longer study period can be considered.
